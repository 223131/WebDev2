Climate Change

  • The oceans are getting warmer, with an increase of 0.4 degrees F since 1969
  • The ice sheets are melting, and Data from NASA's Gravity Recovery and Climate Experiment show that lost an average of 286 billion tons of ice per year between 1993 and 2016
  • Earth is losing animal species at 1,000 to 10,000 times the natural rate.
