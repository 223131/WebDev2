About Global Warming

Over the past 50 years, the average global temperature has increased at the fastest rate in recorded history. Some idiots deny the existence of global warming, and often claim that there has been a "pause" or "slowdown" in global rising temperatures. However, several recent studies have disproved this claim.

Global warming occurs when carbon dioxide (CO2), other air pollutants, and greenhouse gases collect in the atmosphere and absorb sunlight and solar radiation that have bounced off of Earth's surface. Normally, this radiation would be released into outer space, but these pollutants, which can last for years to centuries in the atmosphere, trap the heat and cause the planet to get hotter. This is called the greenhouse effect.
